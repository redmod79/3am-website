## Series Context
This is Study 16, the third study of Arc 3 (The Worship Binary). It addresses the
third angel's warning at full weight — the wrath of God poured out without mixture.
The key challenge: how does "the smoke of their torment ascendeth up for ever and ever"
(Rev 14:11) relate to conditionalist/annihilationist theology? This study must take the
"forever" language seriously while showing that the OT prophetic convention uses identical
language for completed judgments (Isa 34:10 Edom, Jude 7 Sodom). Also addresses thumos
vs orge, akratos (unmixed), "no rest day nor night," and Malachi 4:1-3.
