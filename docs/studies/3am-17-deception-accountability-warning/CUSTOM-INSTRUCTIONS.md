## Series Context
This is Study 17, the final study of Arc 3 (The Worship Binary). It addresses the
justice-of-God questions raised by studies 14-16: Does everyone who receives the mark
end up lost? What about the deceived? Does God guarantee fair warning? Can the mark be
resisted? How does graduated accountability work? Is the close of probation biblical?
This study must handle the tension between divine justice and the severity of the third
angel's warning. Key texts: 2 Thess 2:10-12, Rom 1:24-28, Rev 22:11, Rev 15:8, Dan 12:1,
Luke 12:47-48, Acts 17:30, 1 Tim 1:13, Num 15:22-31.
