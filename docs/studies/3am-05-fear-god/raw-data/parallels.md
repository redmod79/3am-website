# Cross-Testament Parallels

- Rev 14:7 → Eccl 12:13-14 (ot, vocabulary + structural — fear God (yare/phobeo) + commandments (shamar/tereo) + judgment (mishpat/krisis))
- Rev 14:7 → Jonah 1:9 (ot, vocabulary + structural — fear + LORD + God of heaven + Creator of sea (0.383 hybrid score))
- Rev 14:7 → Ps 96:4-9 (ot, vocabulary + structural — fear + glory + worship + Creator + universal scope)
- Rev 14:7 → Deut 6:13 (ot, vocabulary — fear + serve (Jesus quotes as worship in Matt 4:10))
- Rev 14:7 → Deut 10:12-13 (ot, structural — fear-love-serve-obey pattern as what God requires)
- 1 John 4:18 → Ps 130:4 (ot, thematic contrast — 1 John: love casts out fear; Ps 130: forgiveness PRODUCES fear (0.354 hybrid score))
- 1 John 4:18 → Rom 8:15 (nt, vocabulary + thematic — spirit of bondage to phobos vs. spirit of adoption; slavish vs. filial)
- Matt 10:28 → Rev 13:15 (nt, thematic — do not fear those who kill body maps onto do not fear beast's death threat)
- Matt 10:28 → Dan 3:16-18 (ot, thematic — fear of God overrides fear of furnace/death-penalty worship command)
- Matt 10:28 → Isa 8:13 (ot, vocabulary + thematic — make God your fear/mora, not the human threat)
- Eccl 12:13 → 1 John 5:3 (nt, vocabulary + structural — fear God + keep commandments = love of God + keep commandments (0.337 hybrid score))
- Eccl 12:13 → Rev 14:12 (nt, structural — fear God + commandments = patience of saints + commandments of God + faith of Jesus)
- Exod 20:18-20 → Heb 12:18-24 (nt, thematic — Sinai terror contrasted with Zion grace; both involve fear but different kinds)
- Exod 20:18-20 → Rom 8:15 (nt, thematic — dual-fear at Sinai maps onto bondage-fear vs. adoption)
- Prov 1:7 → Col 2:3 (nt, thematic — fear of LORD = beginning of knowledge; in Christ are hid all treasures of wisdom and knowledge)
- Deut 6:13 → Matt 4:10 (nt, quotation — Jesus quotes Deut 6:13, replacing 'fear' with 'worship' (proskyneo))
- Isa 29:13 → Matt 15:8-9 (nt, quotation — Jesus quotes Isa 29:13; false fear → vain worship; connects fear to worship quality)
