3. **The mother-daughter structure: METER TON PORNON:**

The research agent must parse Rev 17:5 thoroughly with greek_parser.py. In Greek, meter + genitive plural normally denotes origin or production (cf. 'mother of the living' in Gen 3:20 LXX). Compare with Ezek 16:44-45 where Jerusalem's 'mother' is established — run hebrew_parser.py on that passage.
