## Series Context
This is Study 8 of a 22-study series on the Three Angels' Messages (Rev 14:6-12).
This study examines "worship him that made heaven, and earth, and the sea, and the
fountains of waters" as the third imperative. It makes the textual case for the Sabbath
connection from within the 3AM text itself by comparing the creation formula with Exodus
20:11 through the LXX. It addresses the objection that the creation call is generic.
This study addresses criticism #6: "The Sabbath connection is just inference."
