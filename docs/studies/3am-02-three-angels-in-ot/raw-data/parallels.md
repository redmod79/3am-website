# Cross-Testament Parallels

- Rev 14:7 → Gen 28:17 (ot, vocabulary (fear, heaven))
- Rev 14:7 → Psa 96:13 (ot, vocabulary + theological (judgment, worship))
- Rev 14:7 → Eccl 12:14 (ot, theological (judgment on every work))
- Gen 6:5 → Matt 15:19 (nt, vocabulary (evil, heart, thought))
- Gen 6:5 → Matt 24:37-39 (nt, typological (Noah's time = end time))
- Exo 12:12 → Num 33:4 (ot, vocabulary + structural (judgment on gods, identical phrase))
- Exo 12:12 → Rev 11:8 (nt, typological (Egypt as apocalyptic symbol))
- Gen 19:24 → Luke 17:29 (nt, vocabulary + typological (fire, brimstone, Sodom, end-time))
- Gen 19:24 → 2 Pet 2:6 (nt, typological (Sodom as example/ensample))
- Gen 19:24 → Jude 1:7 (nt, typological (Sodom as example, eternal fire))
- Gen 19:24 → Rev 14:10 (nt, vocabulary (fire and brimstone in judgment))
- 1 Ki 18:21 → Luke 1:17 (nt, typological (Elijah's spirit, turning hearts))
- Jer 51:6 → Rev 18:4 (nt, vocabulary + structural (come out/flee from Babylon, deliver soul))
- Jer 51:7 → Rev 14:8 (nt, vocabulary + structural (golden cup, nations drunken, Babylon))
- Jer 51:8 → Rev 14:8 (nt, vocabulary (Babylon is fallen, is fallen))
- 2 Pet 2:5 → Gen 6:8-9 (ot, vocabulary + typological (Noah, righteousness))
- Exo 15:1-2 → Rev 15:3-4 (nt, structural (Song of Moses → Song of Moses and the Lamb))
