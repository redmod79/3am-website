## Series Context
This study begins Arc 4 (Result and Defense). It examines hupomone (patient endurance) as the summary response to the three messages. The three angels have proclaimed (Arc 1), exposed (Arc 2), and warned (Arc 3). Now Rev 14:12 describes the CHARACTER of those who respond: "Here is the patience of the saints." This is not passive resignation but active, hopeful perseverance under pressure.
