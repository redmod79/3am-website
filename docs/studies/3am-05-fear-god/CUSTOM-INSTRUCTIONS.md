## Series Context
This is Study 5 of a 22-study series on the Three Angels' Messages (Rev 14:6-12).
This study examines "Fear God" (phobethete ton theon) as the first imperative of the
everlasting gospel. It explores the OT yare/phobos tradition, resolves the tension with
1 John 4:18 ("perfect love casts out fear"), and shows how fearing God is the foundation
for refusing to fear the beast (Rev 13:15, Matt 10:28). It connects to Ecclesiastes 12:13
as the OT template for the first angel's message.
