## Key Finding: oros translated as "hill" — ONLY 3 occurrences
The 3 "hill" translations (Luk 3:5, Luk 4:29, Luk 9:37 range) are debatable contexts. In Revelation specifically:
- Rev 6:14 — "every mountain" (clearly mountain)
- Rev 6:15-16 — "rocks of the mountains" (clearly mountain)
- Rev 8:8 — "a great mountain burning with fire" (clearly mountain)
- Rev 14:1 — "mount Sion" (clearly mountain)
- Rev 16:20 — "the mountains were not found" (clearly mountain)
- Rev 17:9 — "seven mountains" (the disputed verse)
- Rev 21:10 — "a great and high mountain" (clearly mountain)

Every other oros in Revelation unambiguously means "mountain." The seven-hills-of-Rome reading requires oros to shift meaning in Rev 17:9 alone.
