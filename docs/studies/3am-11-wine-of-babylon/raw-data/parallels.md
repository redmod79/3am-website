# Cross-Testament Parallels

- Rev 14:8 → Jer 51:7 (ot, direct allusion — golden cup, nations drunk, agency shift)
- Rev 14:8 → Isa 21:9 (ot, vocabulary — 'Babylon is fallen, is fallen' doubled formula)
- Rev 14:8 → Rev 18:2 (nt, vocabulary + structural — identical doubled aorist formula)
- Rev 14:8 → Rev 16:19 (nt, vocabulary + structural — identical genitive chain with polarity flip)
- Rev 18:23 → Isa 47:9-12 (ot, vocabulary — LXX pharmakeia for Babylon's sorceries)
- Rev 18:23 → Nah 3:4 (ot, thematic — sorcery + fornication as mechanism of national deception)
- Rev 17:4 → Jer 51:7 (ot, vocabulary — golden cup in hand)
- Rev 17:4 → Jer 4:30 (ot, imagery — adorned woman, gold, scarlet)
- Jer 51:7 → Rev 14:8 (nt, direct allusion — same imagery, agency shift from God to Babylon)
- Jer 51:7 → Rev 17:4 (nt, vocabulary — golden cup)
- Rev 18:4 → Jer 51:6 (ot, vocabulary + structural — 'flee/come out of Babylon')
- Rev 18:4 → Jer 51:45 (ot, vocabulary — 'my people, go out' (ammi tse'u → ho laos mou exelthate))
