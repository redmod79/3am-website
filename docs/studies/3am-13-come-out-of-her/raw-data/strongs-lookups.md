## G4167 — poimne (flock)
Critical for John 10:16. Distinct from G833 aule (fold/courtyard). The KJV 'one fold' in John 10:16 mistranslates poimne as 'fold' when it means 'flock.'

## G833 — aule (fold/courtyard)
Used in John 10:16 for the institutional fold (aules tautes — 'this fold'). Distinguished from poimne (G4167, flock). The sheep are not of this AULE but will become one POIMNE.

## G2853 — kollao (glue/join)
Rev 18:5: ekollethesan (V-API-3P, aorist passive) — her sins 'were glued together' reaching heaven. The verb means to cement or join — image of sins piled on sins bonded into a structure reaching heaven.
