## Series Context
This is Study 7 of a 22-study series on the Three Angels' Messages (Rev 14:6-12).
This study examines "the hour of his judgment is come" (hoti elthen he hora tes kriseos autou)
as the reason clause supporting the three imperatives. It addresses whether this judgment is
the cross, the second coming, or the pre-advent/investigative judgment. It traces krisis back
through the LXX to Daniel 7's dina. It addresses the hardest apologetic challenge: how can
judgment be "good news" and whether the pre-advent judgment undermines assurance.
