# Cross-Testament Parallels

- Matt 3:10 → Dan 4:11 (ot, vocabulary (tree, root, judgment))
- Matt 3:10 → Isa 6:13 (ot, vocabulary (burn, tree — judgment fire imagery))
- Matt 3:10 → Jer 17:8 (ot, vocabulary (fruit, root, tree — fruitfulness/judgment))
- Matt 3:10 → Luke 3:9 (nt, synoptic parallel (identical text))
- Matt 3:10 → Matt 7:19 (nt, vocabulary + thematic (bad fruit burned — Jesus repeats John's judgment language))
- Matt 23:37 → Jer 4:11 (ot, vocabulary (Jerusalem judgment))
- Matt 23:37 → 2 Chr 21:13 (ot, vocabulary (Jerusalem, killing))
- Matt 23:37 → Luke 13:34 (nt, synoptic parallel (identical lament))
- Matt 23:37 → Acts 7:52 (nt, thematic (killing prophets — Stephen echoes Jesus' accusation))
- Acts 7:51 → Jer 6:10 (ot, vocabulary (ear, uncircumcised))
- Acts 7:51 → Jer 7:24 (ot, vocabulary (ear, heart — refusal to hear))
- Acts 7:51 → Isa 6:10 (ot, vocabulary (ear, heart — hardened perception))
- Acts 7:51 → Heb 3:7 (nt, thematic (Holy Spirit + resistance — today if you will hear))
- Acts 17:31 → Psa 9:9 (ot, vocabulary (judge, world — God judges the world))
- Acts 17:31 → Psa 96:13 (ot, vocabulary (judge, world — he cometh to judge the earth))
- Acts 17:31 → Psa 98:9 (ot, vocabulary (judge, world — he shall judge the world with righteousness))
- Acts 17:31 → Acts 10:42 (nt, vocabulary + structural (appointed, dead, judge — Christ ordained as Judge))
- Acts 17:31 → 1 Thess 1:10 (nt, thematic (dead, raised — resurrection as proof of coming judgment))
- Rom 2:16 → Eccl 3:17 (ot, vocabulary (judge — God shall judge))
- Rom 2:16 → Dan 2:28 (ot, vocabulary (secret — God reveals hidden things))
- Rom 2:16 → Rom 16:25 (nt, vocabulary (gospel, Christ, Jesus, secret — gospel encompasses hidden things))
- Rom 2:16 → 2 Tim 4:1 (nt, vocabulary (Christ, Jesus, judge — Christ as Judge))
- Rev 14:7 → Gen 28:17 (ot, vocabulary (fear, heaven))
- Rev 14:7 → Jonah 1:9 (ot, vocabulary (fear, heaven, sea — Jonah fears the God who made heaven and sea))
- Rev 14:7 → Rev 19:1 (nt, vocabulary (glory, heaven, loud, voice — heavenly praise/proclamation))
- Rev 14:7 → Rev 15:4 (nt, vocabulary (because, worship — fear and worship God))
- Rev 14:7 → Acts 14:15 (nt, vocabulary (heaven, made, sea — Paul at Lystra: the living God who made heaven, earth, sea))
- Rev 14:7 → Rev 18:10 (nt, vocabulary (fear, hour, judgment — Babylon's judgment in one hour))
