# Cross-Testament Parallels

- Rev 14:7 → Eccl 12:13-14 (ot, vocabulary + structural — fear God + judgment; Ecclesiastes is the OT template the first angel restates universally)
- Rev 14:7 → Rev 15:4 (nt, vocabulary — fear + glorify + worship + judgment; same cluster repeated post-judgment)
- Rev 14:7 → Rev 16:9 (nt, verbal echo — dote auto doxan (14:7 imperative) vs. dounai auto doxan (16:9 infinitive); same lexemes, shift from command to refusal)
- Rev 14:7 → Rev 11:13 (nt, vocabulary — edokan doxan to theo (11:13 positive response) using same didomi+doxa construction)
- Rev 14:7 → Rev 4:11 (nt, vocabulary + structural — heavenly pattern of creation-grounded glory-giving)
- Rev 14:7 → Josh 7:19 (ot, idiom — 'give glory' as confession/judicial oath formula; sim kabod la-YHWH parallels dote doxan)
- Rev 14:7 → Mal 2:2 (ot, vocabulary + structural — latheth kabod lishmi; curse for not giving glory parallels plagues in Rev 16)
- Rev 14:7 → 1 Chr 16:28-29 (ot, vocabulary — habuw la-YHWH kabod; the call to give glory and worship)
- Rev 14:7 → Exod 20:11 (ot, vocabulary — creation language 'made heaven and earth and sea' echoes the Sabbath commandment)
- Rev 14:7 → Acts 17:30-31 (nt, structural — same logic: Creator commands repentance because judgment is appointed)
- Rom 1:21 → Rev 14:7 (nt, verbal inversion — edoxasan (glorified not) inverts dote doxan (give glory); same doxa root)
- Rom 1:21 → Psa 106:20 (ot, vocabulary — 'changed their glory' (Psa 106:20) parallels 'changed the glory' (Rom 1:23))
- Rom 1:21 → Jer 2:11 (ot, vocabulary — 'my people have changed their glory' echoes the glory-exchange pattern)
- 2 Cor 3:18 → Exod 34:29-35 (ot, structural + thematic — Moses' shining face is the OT background for the beholding-transformation mechanism)
- 2 Cor 3:18 → Rom 8:29 (nt, vocabulary — conformed to the image (eikon) of the Son; same image-transformation trajectory)
- John 17:4 → John 4:34 (nt, vocabulary — teleioo in both; Christ's life-purpose statement anticipates the glorification declaration)
- John 17:4 → John 19:30 (nt, vocabulary — tetelestai (G5055 teleo) as the cross's counterpart to John 17:4's completion)
- 1 Cor 6:20 → Lev 10:3 (ot, thematic — glorify God in approaching Him; same obligation to sanctify/glorify in the divine presence)
- Rev 16:9 → Rev 9:20-21 (nt, structural — sixth trumpet refusal to repent anticipates fourth plague refusal to give glory)
- Rev 16:9 → Rev 16:11 (nt, verbal repetition — 'repented not' repeated under fifth plague; escalating refusal)
