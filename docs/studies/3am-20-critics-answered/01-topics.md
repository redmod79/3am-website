# Topics Research

## Question
How do the 14 major critical arguments against the three angels' messages fare when tested against biblical evidence? Take each objection and respond using evidence from the prior 19 studies.

### GOSPEL — GENERAL

- Matt 4:23
- Mark 1:14
- Rom 1:16
- 1 Cor 15:3-4
- Rev 14:6

Gospel topic — covers the good news of salvation through Jesus Christ. Key entries relevant to Rev 14:6 everlasting gospel. See library entry G2098-euangelion for comprehensive data.

### COMMANDMENTS — GENERAL

- Exo 20:1-17
- Matt 22:36-40
- Rom 7:7-12
- Rev 14:12

Commandments topic — covers divine injunctions, particularly the Decalogue. Key entries relevant to Rev 14:12 tas entolas tou theou. See library entry G1785-entole.

### OBEDIENCE — GENERAL

- Gen 26:5
- Rom 8:4
- Gal 5:6
- 1 John 5:3
- Rev 14:12

Obedience topic — relationship of obedience to faith. Covers commandment-keeping as gospel fruit, not works-righteousness. See WG-obedience and WG-commandment-keeping.

### SABBATH — GENERAL

- Exo 20:8-11
- Exo 31:13
- Exo 31:17
- Ezek 20:12
- Heb 4:9

Sabbath topic — the seventh-day rest as sign/seal. Sabbath is the ONLY Decalogue commandment called owth (sign) four times. See H7676-shabbath, G4520-sabbatismos, WG-sabbath.

### WORSHIP — GENERAL

- Exo 20:3-5
- Rev 14:7
- Rev 14:9
- Rev 13:4

Worship topic — proskyneo as central verb in the three angels' messages. Rev 14:7 commands worship of Creator; Rev 14:9 warns against worship of beast. See G4352-proskyneo, WG-worship.

### JUDGMENT — THE GENERAL

- Dan 7:9-10
- Matt 12:36
- Rom 2:16
- 2 Cor 5:10
- Rev 14:7

Judgment topic — the general judgment. Covers both evaluative (krisis) and condemnatory (katakrima) dimensions. See G2920-krisis, G2631-katakrima, WG-judgment.

### ASSURANCE — GENERAL

- Rom 8:1
- Rom 8:33-34
- 1 John 5:13
- Eph 1:13-14

Assurance topic — grounds of certainty for believers. Five bases: God's promises, Spirit's seal, Christ's intercession, books testify for believers, completed work of Christ.

### SEAL — GENERAL

- Rom 4:11
- Eph 1:13
- Eph 4:30
- Rev 7:2-4

Seal topic — divine ownership mark. Covers sphragis (G4973) as authentication, protection, and ownership indicator. See G4973-sphragis, G4972-sphragizo, WG-sealing.

### BABYLON — CITY OF

- Rev 14:8
- Rev 17:1-5
- Rev 18:4-5

Babylon topic — the symbolic harlot city. Zanah/porneia vocabulary requires prior covenant relationship. Harlot-bride bracket (Rev 17:1-3 parallels 21:9-10). See H2181-zanah, G4202-porneia, H1101-balal.

### ETERNAL PUNISHMENT — GENERAL

- Matt 25:46
- Rev 14:10-11
- Rev 20:10

Eternal punishment topic — consequences of sin. Key debate between ECT and annihilationism. See G166-aionios, G165-aion, H5769-owlam, WG-forever, WG-death, WG-destroy.

### FAITH — GENERAL

- Gen 15:6
- Rom 3:31
- Gal 5:6
- Heb 11:1
- Rev 14:12

Faith topic — pistis as trust, faithfulness, and the body of belief. Rev 14:12 pistis Iesou genitive debate (subjective vs. objective). See G4102-pistis, WG-faith, grammar-reference/greek/subjective-objective-genitive.md.

### LAW — GENERAL

- Rom 3:31
- Rom 7:7
- Rom 8:4
- Gal 3:24

Law topic — nomos as Torah/Mosaic law. Distinction from entole in Revelation (nomos never appears in Revelation). See G3551-nomos, WG-law, TR-towrah-nomos.

### SIGN — GENERAL

- Exo 31:13
- Exo 31:17
- Ezek 20:12
- Ezek 20:20
- Rom 4:11

Sign topic — owth as covenantal marker. Sabbath is the only Decalogue commandment designated owth. Rom 4:11 bridges sign (semeion) and seal (sphragis) vocabulary. See H226-owth, WG-sign, TR-owth-semeion.

### ANGEL — CELESTIAL SPIRIT

- Rev 14:6
- Rev 14:8
- Rev 14:9
- Rev 12:1
- Rev 15:1

Angel topic — angelos as messenger. The three angels are symbolic of human proclaimers within an apocalyptic literary framework. Symbolic antagonists (beasts) demand symbolic protagonists (angels). See WG-angel.

### DOOR — GENERAL

- Gen 7:16
- Matt 25:10
- Luke 13:25

Door topic — shut-door motif as close-of-probation precedent. Three texts independently employ the door-closing image.

### PROBATION — GENERAL

- Rev 22:11
- Rev 15:8
- Dan 12:1

Probation topic — finalization of destinies before the Second Coming. Three independent convergence texts.

### PREACHING — GENERAL

- Luke 3:18
- Acts 17:22-31
- Rom 1:1-4
- Rev 14:6

Preaching topic — gospel proclamation that includes judgment. Three texts explicitly label combined message (including judgment) as gospel.

### REPENTANCE — GENERAL

- Acts 17:30
- Matt 3:2
- Rev 14:7

Repentance topic — moral reorientation as gospel content. Paul at Athens commands repentance alongside judgment announcement.

### IDOLATRY — GENERAL

- Rev 13:4
- Rev 14:9
- Rev 14:11
- Acts 17:29

Idolatry topic — false worship contrasted with true worship in Rev 14:7. The charagma (G5480) is linked to proskyneo in every occurrence.

### TORMENTS — GENERAL

- Rev 14:10-11
- Rev 20:10
- Matt 25:46

Torments topic — basanizo vocabulary in Revelation. Subject differentiation: human wicked devoured (katephagen, Rev 20:9); devil/beast/false prophet tormented (Rev 20:10).
