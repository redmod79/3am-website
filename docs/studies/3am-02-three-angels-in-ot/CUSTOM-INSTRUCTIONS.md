## Series Context
This is Study 2 of a 22-study series on the Three Angels' Messages (Rev 14:6-12).
This study proves that the three-part pattern of the three angels' messages — (1) gospel call to
worship the true God, (2) exposure of a false system, (3) warning of judgment — is repeated
throughout OT history. It examines Noah before the flood, Abraham and Sodom, Moses and Egypt,
Elijah at Carmel, Josiah's reform, and Jeremiah's call to leave Babylon. This answers the
critic argument that "the 3AM are a 19th-century Adventist invention."

## Critic Engagement
The web research agent should search for scholarly analysis of warning-before-judgment patterns
in the OT, prophetic warning traditions, and any arguments that the 3AM pattern is unique to
Revelation rather than a recurring biblical motif.
