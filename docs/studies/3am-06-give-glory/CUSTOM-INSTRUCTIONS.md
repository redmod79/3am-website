## Series Context
This is Study 6 of a 22-study series on the Three Angels' Messages (Rev 14:6-12).
This study examines "give glory to him" (dote auto doxan) as the second imperative
of the everlasting gospel. It explores how glory (doxa/kabod) is given to God — through
confession, character, conduct, fruit-bearing, bodily stewardship, and beholding-transformation.
It connects giving glory to the judgment announcement and shows the verbal echo between
Rev 14:7 and Rev 16:9. It examines Christ as the model of giving glory (John 17:4).
