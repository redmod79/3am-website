    Topics Research¶

    Question¶

    Babylon's fall: doubled epesen, proleptic aorist, Isa 21:9 template, Rev 14:8 / Rev 17-18 relationship, Jer 50-51 verbal parallels, 'in one hour' refrain, millstone imagery, progressive fulfillment.

    BABYLON — CITY OF / FIGURATIVE¶

    • Gen 10:10
    • Gen 11:1-9
    • Isa 13:1-22
    • Isa 14:4-26
    • Isa 21:1-10
    • Isa 47:1-15
    • Jer 50:1-46
    • Jer 51:1-64
    • Dan 4:30
    • Dan 5:25-29
    • Rev 14:8
    • Rev 16:19
    • Rev 17:1-18
    • Rev 18:1-24

    CITY OF: Built by Nimrod GEN 10:10. In the land of Shinar GEN 10:10; 11:2. Tower of GEN 11:1-9. Capital of the kingdom DAN 4:30; 2KI 25:13. Gates of ISA 45:1,2; JER 51:58. Walled JER 51:44,58. Splendor of ISA 14:4. Peter writes from 1PE 5:13. Prophecies concerning PSA 87:4; 137:8,9; ISA 13; 14:4-26; 21:1-10; 46:1,2; 47; 48:14,20; JER 21:4-10; 25:12-14; 50; 51; EZK 21:19; 26; DAN 2:21-38; 4:10-26; 5:25-29; 7; HAB 1:5-11; ZEC 2:7-9. FIGURATIVE REV 14:8; 16:19; 17; 18. EMPIRE OF: Founded by Nimrod GEN 10:10. Called LAND OF SHINAR, SHESHACH, MERATHAIM, CHALDEA. Prophetic denunciations against PSA 137:8,9; ISA 13; 14:21; 43:14-17; 47; JER 50; 51.

    MILLSTONE — GENERAL¶

    • Deut 24:6
    • Judg 9:53
    • Matt 18:6
    • Mark 9:42
    • Rev 18:21-22

    Not to be taken in pledge DEU 24:6. Abimelech killed by one JUDG 9:53. Figurative MAT 18:6; REV 18:21-22.

    WINE — FIGURATIVE / ABOMINATIONS¶

    • Psa 75:8
    • Isa 51:17
    • Jer 25:15-17
    • Jer 51:7
    • Rev 14:8
    • Rev 14:10
    • Rev 16:19
    • Rev 17:2
    • Rev 18:3

    Cup of fury in God's hand PSA 75:8. Cup of trembling ISA 51:17. Cup of wrath given to nations JER 25:15-17. Golden cup in Babylon's hand JER 51:7. Wine of wrath of her fornication REV 14:8. Wine of wrath of God REV 14:10. Cup of fierceness of his wrath REV 16:19.

    TYRE — PROPHECIES / COMMERCE¶

    • Isa 23:8-17
    • Ezek 26:13
    • Ezek 27:1-36

    Merchants as princes ISA 23:8. Silenced music EZK 26:13. Cargo list and trade inventory EZK 27:12-25. Lamentation over Tyre EZK 27:26-36.

    WATCHMAN — GENERAL¶

    • Isa 21:6-9
    • Jer 51:12
    • Hab 2:1
    • Ezek 33:1-7

    Set a watchman to declare what he sees ISA 21:6-9. Watchmen set against Babylon JER 51:12. Habakkuk stands on his watch HAB 2:1.
