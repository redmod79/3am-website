    Bible Study: How do the 14 major critical arguments against the three angels' messages fare when tested against biblical evidence? Take each objection and respond using evidence from the prior 19 studies.¶

    Question¶

    How do the 14 major critical arguments against the three angels' messages fare when tested against biblical evidence? Take each objection and respond using evidence from the prior 19 studies.

    Prior Research Summary¶

    This is study 20 of 21 in the 3AM series. All 19 prior studies are complete (3am-00 through 3am-19). The reference agent gathered findings from every prior study mapped to each of the 14 objections, plus external corpus leads from EGW, Secrets Unsealed, and YouTube critics (Answering Adventism). Web research identified the strongest formulations of each objection from three critical traditions: former-Adventist (Ratzlaff, Tinker), evangelical/Reformed (Packer, Ortlund, GotQuestions), and academic/internal (Ford, deSilva, Spectrum). The strongest objections are: entole vs. nomos (Obj 4/6), Ford's Glacier View arguments (Obj 8), Sabbath silence (Obj 6), ECT arguments (Obj 7), and Gal 1:8-9 application (Obj 2). The weakest are: no gospel (Obj 1), literal angels (Obj 11), and 19th-century invention (Obj 14).

    Focus Areas¶

    1. Objection 1-2: Is the everlasting gospel THE gospel or 'another gospel'?: READ $WORD_STUDIES/G2098-euangelion.md and $WORD_STUDIES/G2097-euangelizo.md and TR-basar-euangelizo.md for the gospel vocabulary chain. The critical question is whether Paul's gospel is limited to 1 Cor 15:3-4 or broader — check Rom 1:1-4, Acts 17:22-31, Gal 3:8 for Paul's OWN variant summaries. Run greek_parser.py --verse on Rev 14:6-7 to confirm the legon participle embeds v.7 as gospel CONTENT. Compare Acts 17:22-31 element-by-element with Rev 14:6-7 (Creator God, universal scope, judgment, moral reorientation). Check Gal 1:6 for heteros (qualitatively different) — does Rev 14 add works-righteousness (the specific thing Paul condemns) or creation-worship? Retrieve Ecc 12:13-14 as the OT structural twin. Ratzlaff conflates Rev 14's angels with EGW's 'accompanying angel' — the research agent should note this conflation but focus on the biblical text. Verses: Rev 14:6-7, Gal 1:6-9, Acts 17:22-31, 1 Cor 15:3-4, Rom 1:1-4, Gal 3:8, Ecc 12:13-14, Rom 2:16, Luke 3:18, 2 Tim 1:9 Strong's: G2098, G2097, G166, H1319, G5399, G2920

    2. Objection 3: Do the apostles teach these elements as gospel?: Three independent NT texts explicitly label the combined message — including judgment — as gospel: Luke 3:18 (euengelizeto for John's judgment preaching), Rom 2:16 ('according to my gospel'), Rev 14:6-7 (judgment IS the everlasting gospel content). Paul at Athens (Acts 17:22-31) contains the tightest correspondence with Rev 14:6-7. Run cross_testament_parallels_v2.py --hybrid-nt on Rev 14:7 to find NT parallels.
