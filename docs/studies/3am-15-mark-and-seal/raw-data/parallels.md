# Cross-Testament Parallels

- Rev 7:3 → Ezek 9:4 (ot, structural + vocabulary — both: marking on foreheads, judgment on unmarked, begins at sanctuary, universal scope, marking precedes destruction)
- Rev 14:7 → Exo 20:11 (ot, vocabulary — four shared terms in sequence: poieo/asah (made), ouranos/shamayim (heaven), ge/erets (earth), thalassa/yam (sea))
- Rev 14:7 → Jonah 1:9 (ot, vocabulary — fear + heaven + sea shared terminology (hybrid score 0.383))
- Ezek 9:4 → Rev 7:2-3 (nt, structural — tav on foreheads (OT) becomes seal on foreheads (NT); both precede judgment)
- Deut 6:8 → Rev 13:16 (nt, structural — sign on hand + frontlets between eyes (OT) becomes mark on hand or forehead (NT); the beast inverts the loyalty pattern)
- Exo 31:13 → Jhn 9:16 (nt, vocabulary — sabbath + keep + sign shared (hybrid score 0.371))
- Rom 4:11 → Gen 17:11 (ot, vocabulary + conceptual — circumcision as owth (Gen 17:11) = semeion + sphragis (Rom 4:11); the owth-sign-seal bridge)
- Heb 4:9 → Rev 14:11 (nt, thematic irony — sabbatismos remains (Heb 4:9) vs. no anapausis for beast-worshippers (Rev 14:11))
- Ezek 8:16 → Ezek 9:4 (ot, structural contrast — sun worship (greatest abomination, 8:16) vs. tav on faithful (9:4); immediately sequential)
- Rev 14:1 → Rev 7:3 (nt, identification — Father's name on foreheads (14:1) = content of the seal of the living God (7:3))
