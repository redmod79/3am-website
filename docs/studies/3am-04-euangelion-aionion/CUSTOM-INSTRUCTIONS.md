## Series Context
This is Study 4 of a 22-study series on the Three Angels' Messages (Rev 14:6-12).
This begins Arc 1: The First Angel. This study examines "everlasting gospel" (euangelion aionion)
in Rev 14:6 — what it means, whether it's the same gospel Paul preached, the anarthrous
construction, the basar-euangelizo LXX bridge, the imperial euangelia parallel, and whether
it passes the Galatians 1:6-9 test. GSP-10 proved the 3AM ARE the gospel; this study goes
deeper into the specific phrase "everlasting gospel" and its theological implications.
