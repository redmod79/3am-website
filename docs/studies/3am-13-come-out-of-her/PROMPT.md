9. **The speaker identity and the voice from heaven:**
Rev 18:4 introduces the speaker as 'allon phonen ek tou ouranou' — another voice from heaven. READ `$WORD_STUDIES/G5456-phone.md` for the voice vocabulary in Revelation. The 'allon' (another, of the same kind) distinguishes this voice from the angel of 18:1 but still locates it in heaven. The possessive 'ho laos MOU' (MY people) is a covenant designation — in the OT, only God uses this phrase in the possessive first person. Bratcher & Hatton note: 'The words my people clearly identify the speaker as God himself (cross-ref 16:1).' But in Revelation, angelic messengers regularly speak on God's behalf.
