# The Three Angels' Messages: What Does the Bible Teach?

*A comprehensive 22-study biblical investigation of Revelation 14:6-12 -- the three angels' messages. Examines every phrase, every theological connection, every major objection, and every word in the original Greek.*

---

## The Question

What are the three angels' messages of Revelation 14:6-12? Are they the gospel or something else? What do they require? How do they connect to the rest of Scripture? And how do the 14 major objections against them hold up under examination?

## The Approach

Each study uses the bible-study8 pipeline: web research for scholarly context, scope discovery through topical dictionaries and concordances, original-language analysis via Hebrew and Greek parsers, cross-testament parallel discovery, and a verification pass that machine-checks every Strong's number and occurrence count in the final prose. No claim goes unchecked.
