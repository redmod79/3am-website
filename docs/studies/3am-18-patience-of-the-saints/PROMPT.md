## Prior Research Summary
Five prior studies provide direct context: 3am-14 (worship binary — the crisis endurance responds to), 3am-16 (wrath without mixture — the severity endurance faces), 3am-17 (deception/accountability — the deception endurance penetrates), nikao-overcoming-chain (the vocabulary coupling with hupomone), and revs2-08 (overcomer promises). Two canonical word studies exist: G5281-hupomone (32 NT uses, 7 in Revelation, etymology hupo+meno = 'staying under') and G3528-nikao (28 NT uses, 17 in Revelation). Web research found Meyers (2018) arguing 'enduring resistance' as translation, List (2022) on Job's endurance in Greco-Roman context, and multiple Adventist/commentary sources. External corpus (EGW, Bohr) identifies hupomone as 'independent, unyielding, defiant perseverance' and distinguishes it from makrothumia. The hode formula in Rev 13:10 and 14:12 creates an inclusio bracketing the third angel's message.
