# Hebrew Parsing Raw Data

## Gen 11:9
Hebrew: עַל־כֵּן קָרָא שְׁמָהּ בָּבֶל כִּי־שָׁם בָּלַל יְהוָה שְׂפַת כָּל־הָאָרֶץ
Key: בָּבֶל (babel, PropN) — wordplay with בָּלַל (balal, Verb.Qal.Perf.3ms, "confound/moisten")
Gloss: "Therefore the name of it was called Babel, because there YHWH confounded (balal) the language of all the earth"

## Ezek 16:15
Hebrew: וַתִּבְטְחִי בְיָפְיֵךְ וַתִּזְנִי עַל־שְׁמֵךְ וַתִּשְׁפְּכִי אֶת־תַּזְנוּתַיִךְ עַל־כָּל־עֹובֵר
Key: תִּזְנִי (zanah, Verb.Qal.Wayq.2fs — "you played the harlot"); תַּזְנוּתַיִךְ (taznuwth, Noun.fp.Abs.+2fs — "your fornications")
Context: Jerusalem addressed as unfaithful bride — zanah vocabulary presupposes covenant marriage

## Hos 1:2
Hebrew: כִּי־זָנֹה תִזְנֶה הָאָרֶץ מֵאַחֲרֵי יְהוָה
Key: זָנֹה (zanah, Verb.Qal.InfAbs — infinitive absolute) + תִזְנֶה (zanah, Verb.Qal.Impf.3fs — imperfect)
The doubled construction (infinitive absolute + imperfect) = emphatic habitual action: "the land has utterly committed whoredom departing from YHWH"

## Isa 1:21
Hebrew: אֵיכָה הָיְתָה לְזֹונָה קִרְיָה נֶאֱמָנָה
Key: לְזֹונָה (zanah, Verb.Qal.Ptcp.fs — "to a harlot"); קִרְיָה (qiryah, "city"); נֶאֱמָנָה (aman, Verb.Niphal.Ptcp.fs — "faithful")
Gloss: "How has the faithful city become a harlot!"

## Exo 28:36
Hebrew: וְעָשִׂיתָ צִּיץ זָהָב טָהוֹר וּפִתַּחְתָּ עָלָיו פִּתּוּחֵי חֹתָם קֹדֶשׁ לַיהוָה
Key: קֹדֶשׁ (qodesh, Noun.ms.Abs — "holiness"); לַיהוָה (YHWH)
Gloss: "Make a plate of pure gold, and engrave upon it like the engravings of a signet: HOLINESS TO THE LORD"
Compare: Rev 17:5 has MYSTERY BABYLON on the harlot's forehead — deliberate inversion

## Jer 51:7
Hebrew: כֹּוס־זָהָב בָּבֶל בְּיַד־יְהוָה מְשַׁכֶּרֶת כָּל־הָאָרֶץ מִיֵּינָהּ שָׁתוּ גֹויִם
Key: כֹּוס (kows, Noun.fs.Cst — "cup"); זָהָב (zahab, "gold"); מְשַׁכֶּרֶת (shakar, Verb.Piel.Ptcp.fs — "making drunk"); יֵּינָהּ (yayin+3fs — "her wine")
Gloss: "Babylon has been a golden cup in YHWH's hand, making all the earth drunk; the nations have drunk of her wine; therefore the nations are mad"
