# Cross-Testament Parallels

- Rev 14:7 → Exo 20:11 (ot, vocabulary + structural — direct creation formula quotation (poieo/asah, ouranon/shamayim, gen/erets, thalassan/yam))
- Rev 14:7 → Eccl 12:13-14 (ot, vocabulary + structural — exact triad match (fear God + commandments + judgment))
- Rev 14:7 → Dan 7:9-10 (ot, vocabulary — krisis/dina/mishpat chain; judgment throne scene)
- Rev 14:7 → Acts 4:24 (nt, vocabulary — same Creator-formula idiom (made heaven, earth, sea))
- Rev 14:7 → Acts 14:15 (nt, vocabulary — 'living God, which made heaven and earth and sea')
- Rev 14:7 → Rev 15:4 (nt, vocabulary + thematic — fear, glorify, nations, worship, judgments (same vocabulary cluster))
- Rev 14:8 → Isa 21:9 (ot, direct quotation — doubled 'fallen, fallen, Babylon' (naphlah naphlah → epesen epesen))
- Rev 14:8 → Jer 51:7-8 (ot, vocabulary + thematic — golden cup, nations drunk with wine, Babylon fallen)
- Rev 14:10 → Gen 19:24-25 (ot, thematic — fire and brimstone judgment pattern (Sodom destruction))
- Rev 14:10 → Psa 75:8 (ot, thematic — cup of wrath tradition ('in the hand of the LORD there is a cup'))
- Rev 14:14 → Dan 7:13 (ot, vocabulary + structural — 'like the Son of man' + cloud (homoion huion anthropou / hos huios anthropou))
- Rev 14:19-20 → Joel 3:13 (ot, vocabulary + structural — sickle, harvest ripe, winepress ('put ye in the sickle'))
- Rev 14:19-20 → Isa 63:1-6 (ot, thematic — winepress of divine judgment, blood-stained garments)
- Rev 14:1 → Rev 7:1-4 (nt, structural — 144,000 sealed (sphragis) in foreheads, same group)
- Rev 14:1 → Rev 13:16-17 (nt, antithetical — Father's name in foreheads (14:1) vs. mark in forehead/hand (13:16))
- Rev 14:12 → Rev 12:17 (nt, structural — same two marks: commandments of God + testimony/faith of Jesus)
- Rev 14:12 → Rev 13:10 (nt, structural — hupomone inclusio (13:10 opening → 14:12 closing, expanded))
- Rev 14:12 → Eccl 12:13-14 (ot, vocabulary + structural — fear God + commandments + judgment triad)
- Rev 14:11 → Isa 34:10 (ot, vocabulary — 'smoke ascending for ever' (identical language, Edom = completed destruction))
- Rev 14:13 → Heb 4:9 (nt, thematic — sabbatismos rest for God's people; connects to the rest/no-rest antithesis)
