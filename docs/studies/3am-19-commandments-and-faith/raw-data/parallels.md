- Rev 14:12 → Deut 28:9 (ot, vocabulary (commandment + keep))
- Rev 14:12 → Dan 9:4 (ot, vocabulary (commandment + keep))

- Rev 12:17 → Rev 12:13 (nt, narrative continuity (dragon, woman))

- Rom 1:5 → Rom 15:18 (nt, vocabulary (obedience, Gentiles))

- Rev 14:12 → Eccl 12:13-14 (ot, structural + theological (fear God + keep commandments + judgment template))
