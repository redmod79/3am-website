## Series Context
This is Study 1 of a 22-study series on the Three Angels' Messages (Rev 14:6-12).
This study establishes the literary structure of Revelation 12-14 and shows how the placement
of the three angels' messages within this macro-structure determines their meaning. It addresses
the cosmic conflict of Rev 12, the two beasts of Rev 13, the Lamb-on-Zion unit (14:1-5),
the three angels (14:6-12), and the harvest (14:14-20). It also addresses whether the angels
are literal or symbolic (the preterist/symbolic reading objection).
